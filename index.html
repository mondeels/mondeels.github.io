AboutMondeels Eximport

Based in Bengaluru

Mondeels Eximport has been serving the fresh and cut flower markets in the Middle East since 2004. We traditionally source our products from reliable farms in the outskirts of the beautiful Garden City Bengaluru.

India, being a tropical country, produces some of the finest agricultural products in the world, and in large scale. We invite you to continue exploring our website and particularly the gallery. If you are interested to get in touch with us, please see the contact section.

We sell our products under the trademarks Blumonarc Flowers, Blumonarc Vegetables and Blumonarc Fruits.
